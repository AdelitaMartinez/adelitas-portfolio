Purpose, Audience & Goal

Purpose:

This project will be a portfolio website to showcase my development abilities to potential employers in hopes of finding employment.

Audience:

This professional website is being made to reach those within the tech industry. This portfolio will specifically target peers, collaborators, recruiting coordinators and employers.

Goal:

The main goal of this portfolio is to showcase my abilities and provide a professional way for employers to get in contact with me via contact form. This portfolio will be a space for built projects to be displayed. This site will also serve as a gateway for potential collaborators and will have connections to my other profiles (linkedIn, github).

Name: Josh Smith

Profession:

Josh is a recruiting coordinator to a small local tech company looking for potential candidates to fill a few entry-level positions. They are looking for both front end and back end candidates.

Technology:

Josh uses his personal mac laptop for work. His preferred browser is chrome. He owns an Iphone 14 which he uses for work at times as well.

Behaviors and Attitudes:

Josh has a very demanding and fast-paced job and is responsible for making quick decision. He has learned how to efficiently skim through large quantities of resumes and applications and can make a decision on a candidate within a few minutes. He is very active on LinkedIn and will sometimes attend local tech meetups.

Frustrations and Needs:

Josh must look at multiple resumes and potential candidates in a short amount of time. He feels like the resumes he's getting are all very similar looking, so he prefers looking at candidates previous work and personal portfolios. He needs to find candidates who have a good personality and is able to work well as part of a team. Josh does not want to have to hunt for skill sets or contact information on a portfolio. He needs to be able to find the information he is looking for easily so that he does not waste time.

Goals:

His only goal is to find a candidates who not only has the technical abilities he is looking for, but also will mesh well with current staff. He would like to see unique portfolios that show both personality and skill. He needs to be able to skim at a portfolio for a few minutes and decide if they are a potential candidate or not. He must also be able to find contact information and links to other socials easily.

User Story:

As a user, I need to quickly figure out if this person is right for this position.
