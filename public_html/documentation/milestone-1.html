Purpose, Audience & Goal

Purpose:

This project will be a portfolio website to showcase my programming abilities.

Audience:

This website is targeted towards others in the tech industry. This website will target peers, recruiting coordinators and employers.

Goal:

The main goal of this portfolio is to showcase my abilities and provide a creative and professional way for employers to get in contact with me via contact form. This site will also serve as a gateway for potential collaborators and will have connections to my other profiles (linkedin, github).

Name: Josh Smith

Profession:

Josh is a recruiting coordinator to a small local tech company looking for potential candidates to fill an entry-level position. They are looking for both front end and back end candidates.

Technology:

Uses Mac at work and at home. His preferred browser is chrome.

Frustrations and Needs:

Josh must look at multiple resumes and potential candidates in a very short amount of time. He feels like the resumes he's getting are all very similar looking, so he prefers looking at previous work and personal portfolios. He needs to find candidates who have a good personality and is able to work well as part of a small team.

Goals:

His main goal is to find a potential candidates who will mesh well with current staff. He would like to see unique portfolios that show both personality and skill. He needs to be able to look at a portfolio for a few minutes and decide if they are a potential candidate or not. He must also be able to find contact information and links to other socials easily and quickly.

User Story:

As a user, I need to quickly figure out if this person is right for this entry-level position.
